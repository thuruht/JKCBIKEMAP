# Jojo's KC Bike Map — Sensitivity Policy

## Purpose

Jojo's KC Bike Map is valuable because it captures route knowledge that standard maps ignore. Some of that knowledge lives in a legal, informal, or situational gray area. The goal is to preserve useful rider intelligence without unnecessarily exposing fragile shortcuts or access points.

## Classification levels

### Official

Safe to show precisely and publicly.

### Informal

Publicly visible, but phrasing should remain restrained and practical.

### Sensitive

Public should see generalized geometry and softened wording. Exact details are admin-only.

### Private

Never rendered publicly.

## Public-facing language guidance

Prefer:

- informal connector
- field-used cut
- unofficial access path
- barrier-crossing option
- weather-dependent connector

Avoid public labels that explicitly advertise fence breaches or other vulnerable access details.

## Rendering guidance

Sensitive features should support:

- generalized public map geometry;
- precise admin-only geometry;
- public-safe summary;
- internal admin note;
- per-feature visibility rules.

## Principle

Map what riders actually use, but disclose sensitive access with restraint.
