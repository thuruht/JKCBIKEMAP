How to use this map

Think in corridors, not perfect routes. Brush Creek is your east-west spine; Blue River is your north-south spine; parks, bridges, and neighborhoods help you stitch the rest together.

Caution: Hand-built exploration map, not turn-by-turn navigation. Routes and conditions subject to rain, mud, snow, flooding, stray dogs, automobile traffic, law enforcement and other hazards.
